Hostel Notice for Students

Welcome to Our Hostel!

Dear students, please take note of the following rules and guidelines:

  • Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. Please respect your fellow students' need for sleep.
  • Use of common areas such as the study hall and recreational areas should be done responsibly and in accordance with posted schedules.
  • Maintenance requests should be submitted to the hostel office or through the designated online form. Please describe the issue clearly.
  • Regular room inspections will be conducted to ensure cleanliness and compliance with hostel regulations.
  • All students must check out of their rooms promptly at the end of the semester or academic year as per hostel policies.

Thank you for your cooperation in maintaining a conducive environment for all students in the hostel.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact the hostel office.
